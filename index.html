I am a Posdoc Research Fellow in LARC (Living Analytics Research Centre), School of Computing and Information Systems, Singapore Management University. I have several publications appeared in top conferences such as ICLR, KDD and MM. My research interests include object detection, face detection and semantic segmentation. I have served as the PC member for top-tier conferences including ICLR, NeuriPS, ICML, CVPR and the invited reviewer for prestigious journals such as TIST and PR. I obtained my B.A from Zhejiang University and Ph.D from Singapore Management University.
